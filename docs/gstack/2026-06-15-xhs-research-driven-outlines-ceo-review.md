## Accepted Scope Expansions

1. Research confidence and warnings.
   - Include sample quality, failed keywords, partial provider failure, and low
     sample confidence in the compact research summary.
2. Compliance-aware source transparency.
   - Show source metadata only: note ID/URL, truncated title, interaction
     summary, and match reason.
   - Do not display full copied note content, source images, or raw provider
     payloads in the frontend by default.
3. Low sample counts still generate editable outlines.
   - Sparse samples reduce confidence and add warnings.
   - They do not hard-block generation because the user can review and edit the
     outlines.

## Deferred

1. Frontend deep research mode switch.
   - Backend and persisted data should support `quick | deep`.
   - First frontend flow stays fixed to `quick` until latency and provider
     reliability are understood.
2. Full connector platform, Approach C.
   - Separate future scope: `packages/xhs-connector`, Cookie/account management,
     SDK adapter, task records, health checks, media cache, and publishing.

## Not In Scope

- Direct Spider_XHS or XHS_ALL_IN_ONE integration inside NestJS.
- Cookie import or encrypted Cookie storage.
- Connector account dashboard.
- Background queue.
- Global content library.
- Raw note list selection UI.

- Direct Xiaohongshu publishing.
- Automatic fallback from `custom` to TikHub.

## Product Risks

### R1: Research can become a fake label

If provider search fails or returns very weak data, the app must not imply the
outline is strongly research-backed. The result should still be useful, but
marked as low confidence or fallback.

Decision: generate editable outlines with clear low-confidence warnings when
samples are weak. Provider setup failures stay hard errors.

### R2: UI can become a search console

The product value is one idea to usable content. The research panel must stay
compact. Raw notes, per-note selectors, and content library behavior are out of
scope.

Decision: show summary, confidence, warnings, and compliant source metadata only.

### R3: Connector scope can swallow the feature

The custom connector platform is a real product track, but not this increment.

Decision: define the connector contract now and defer the connector platform to
`TODOS.md`.
